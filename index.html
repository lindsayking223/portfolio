About

“I believe healing and organizing are the same muscle — both ask us to show up for people, again and again, even when it's slow.”

I'm an early-career nonprofit professional working across community health, grassroots organizing, and mental health support. My path has taken me from direct service to program coordination, always circling back to the same question: what does it actually take for a community to take care of itself?

I'm currently exploring graduate programs that will sharpen that work — public health, social work, or a related field — and building a body of writing and projects alongside it. This site is a living snapshot of that.
